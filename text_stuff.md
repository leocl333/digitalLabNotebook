# List and bullet points

To create list, begin a line with asterisk, or minus:

* Item 1

- Item 1

You can make sub points with 2 spaces before a point:

* Item 1
  * sub item 1
  
* Item 2

If you want to create numbered list:

1. Item 1
1. Item 2
